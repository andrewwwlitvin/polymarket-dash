What is Polymarket & how accurate is it?

Polymarket is a prediction market where prices reflect crowd-implied probabilities. While not perfect, markets often react faster than traditional polls.

Use this site to explore momentum, near-50% setups, and underround signals that may indicate potential mispricings. Always do your own research.

Note: Nothing here is financial advice. Markets are risky; never risk funds you cannot afford to lose.
